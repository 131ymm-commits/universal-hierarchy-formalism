### Core Concept
Reality is organized hierarchically. Each level (quantum, molecular, biological, social, civilizational) has its own characteristic time scale $\tau_i$, dimensionality $n_i$, noise temperature $T_i$, and dissipation coefficient $\gamma_i$.

All levels follow the same dynamical principle:

$$\frac{d\mathbf{x}_i}{dt} = -\gamma_i \nabla F_i + \beta_i \nabla \mathcal{G}_i + \text{noise}$$

where $F_i$ is Free Energy and $\mathcal{G}_i$ is Information Gain.

### Major New Idea: Cross-Level Collapse

**Wave function collapse** is not a fundamental postulate, but an **emergent phenomenon** that occurs when a low-level system interacts with a much higher-level system ($\Delta i \gg 0$).

- Low level (quantum) + High level (macro apparatus) → rapid information compression → loss of interference → classical outcome.
- Same level ($\Delta i \approx 0$) → coherence and interference are preserved.
